Thimbl-CLI quickstart: from zero to hero in 60 seconds. 

Create a post:
   thimbl post "First post"

Follow someone:
   thimbl follow "dk" "dk@telekommunisten.net"

Fetch messages of people you are following:
   thimbl fetch

Print information about how thiml is configured:
   thimbl info

Fine-tuning your setup:
   thimbl setup
You will be prompted for settings. Sensible defaults are offered.
See the full list of commands.
