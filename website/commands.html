Thimbl-CLI commands.

fetch
Retrieve a the messages of people that you are following.

follow NAME ADDRESS
Follow someone. E.g. thimbl follow dk dk@telekommunisten.org

info
Print information about the current configuration of Thimbl-CLI

help
Print some help information. It is not of much help at the moment.

post MESG
Post a message. E.g. thimbl post "Hello world"

print
Print a list of messages in the cache, including your own. You probably want to fetch the messages of the people you follow.

read
Fetch and print messages. It is the equivalent of fetch followed by print

setup
Configure your profile. You will be prompted for the settings.
